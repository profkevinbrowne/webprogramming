Common Cold

The common cold is a viral infectious disease of the upper respiratory tract.

Symptoms

The typical symptoms of a cold include cough, runny nose, sneezing, nasal congestion, and a sore throat.

Other symptoms include:

  • muscle ache
  • fatigue
  • headache
  • loss of appetite

Causes

Viruses

The common cold can be caused by many different viruses, including:

  1. rhinovirus (~30-80%)
  2. coronaviruses (~15%)
  3. influenza viruses (~10-15%)

Transmission

The common cold virus can be transmitted via airborne droplets.


The common cold may be transmitted via contaminated objects.
